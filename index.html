About Me

I was always interested in photography, but of course I started as almost everyone these days. My first photos were taken with my mobile phone. They haven't been anything special, but I started to feel that I might be passionate for it. At the end of the year 2020 I started to think that mobile photography wasn't filling me as I wanted. So I started to learn about cameras and equipment necessary for everyday photography. And after just a half a year I had my first proper camera in my hands.

As the time passed I started to be interested not only in digital photography, but also in classic analog. And now I use canon 200D Mark ii camera on photoshoots,events,family .
